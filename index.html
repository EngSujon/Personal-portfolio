About

I'm a multimedia specialist working at the intersection of broadcast journalism and brand storytelling. My background spans newsroom video editing, green-screen studio production, and corporate digital campaigns — giving me an eye for pacing that keeps a viewer watching and a brand that stays consistent frame to frame.

Currently I lead digital video and motion content at LankaBangla Securities, after cutting my teeth producing daily broadcast packages and documentaries for Daily Samakal, Daily Jaijaidin and Naya Shatabdi. Alongside media, I'm completing a B.Sc in Civil Engineering — a second discipline that keeps my eye for structure sharp.
